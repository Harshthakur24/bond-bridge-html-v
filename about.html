Our Values Section

OUR VALUES

BondBridge Has Anonymity As A CORE Principle

At BondBridge we believe that true connections thrive as users are free to express ideas and make real connections without pressure. Our platform is a secure place for those seeking friendships without the usual social awkwardness.

BondBridge allows users to connect over common ideas, hobbies and interests directly but also anonymously. The BondChat connects people, suggests friends and groups and communicates with users to help alleviate loneliness.

Our Mission

To provide users with an AI-driven, media platform where people can be social, in a secure, judgement free environment, and where ideas can flow freely and openly.

Our Vision

To create a product that allows users from all backrounds and interests to connect in non-judgemental platforms in an effort to assist in reducing widespread loneliness.
